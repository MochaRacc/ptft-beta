Southmead Line

Buses replace trains between Esplanade Street & Southmead

until September 29th

 

Buses are replacing trains between Esplanade Street & Southmead as we conduct major works on the line.
Services on the line will be suspended for this long period as we work to remove level crossings, elevate stations and improve current trackwork.

FAQ

Why are these works happening?

The Southmead Line has been requiring works since we elevate stations between Bradfield & Esplanade Street stations back in 2023, we are continuing those works from where we left off by elevating more stations, including but not limited to Bradfield, Hallfern, Princess Creek, Maynard & Southmead itself.

How will I be affected?

Check in with station staff or lookout for station signage for information, how you are affected will be determined per station. Customers for City Loop services will not be affected.

What is not permitted on Rail Replacement buses?

In compliance of the Public Transport (Compliance, complaints and miscellaneous) Act of 2004, skateboards, bicycles, uncaged pets and e-scooters are not permitted on our Rail Replacement buses
If you are found violating these rules, Transnet Officers can fine you up to $75,000 per violation.

Are the buses accessable?

All rail replacement buses operated by Zyra, McKian's & Whitfords are accessable and disability friendly, make sure you look out for the accessible symbol at the front or doorside
